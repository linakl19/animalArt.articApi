Animal Art Showcase

Welcome to Animal Art Showcase, your gateway to exploring the mesmerizing world of animal-themed art! Utilizing the vast collection of the Art Institute of Chicago, this website brings you a curated selection of artworks featuring animals. Whether you're drawn to majestic lions, playful dolphins, or exotic birds, simply click on the buttons labeled with different animal names to discover stunning pieces of art that celebrate these creatures. Immerse yourself in the beauty and diversity of animal art from various periods and styles, all in one place.

Explore, admire, and let the artistry inspire you!
